Our Facilities

Swimming Pool

Dive into relaxation with our crystal-clear swimming pool surrounded by comfortable lounge chairs and tropical vibes. Perfect for both leisure swims and afternoon sunbathing.

Fitness Gym

Stay in shape during your stay at our modern gym. It features state-of-the-art machines, free weights, and a motivating environment for your daily workout routine.

Spa & Massage

Rejuvenate your mind and body with calming spa treatments and professional massages designed to melt away stress and restore balance.

Restaurant

Experience gourmet dining with a mix of local and international flavors crafted by our talented chefs. Every meal is a feast for the senses.

Sauna

Relax and detoxify in our warm, aromatic sauna rooms — a perfect way to unwind after a long day or workout.

Outdoor Volleyball

Enjoy the sunshine with a fun and energetic game of volleyball on our outdoor sand court, perfect for groups and family bonding.
